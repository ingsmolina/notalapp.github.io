FAQ

How do I activate Notal Pro?

Open the app, try a Pro feature (for example, type = in a table cell) and the subscription screen will appear.

How do I restore purchases?

On the subscription screen, tap “Restore purchases”. You must use the same Apple ID as the original purchase.

How does iCloud sync work?

With Notal Pro, your notes sync automatically using iCloud. You need to be signed in to iCloud on your devices.

Which devices are supported?

Requires iOS 16 or later.
